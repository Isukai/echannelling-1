eChannelling 

eChannelling is an innovative online platform that revolutionizes the way people access healthcare services in Sri Lanka. It provides a convenient and efficient way to book medical appointments with doctors and specialists. By leveraging technology, eChannelling connects patients with healthcare professionals, streamlining the appointment booking process and reducing waiting times. With advanced features like online consultations, electronic prescriptions, and secure payment options, eChannelling aims to enhance the overall healthcare experience, making it more accessible, convenient, and patient-centric.
